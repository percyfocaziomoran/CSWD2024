About P1P0

Welcome to P1P0!

At P1P0, we are passionate about bringing people together through the exciting and dynamic sport of ping pong. Located in the heart of Cork, Ireland, our club is dedicated to providing a welcoming environment where players of all ages and skill levels can enjoy and improve their game.

Our Mission

To promote the sport of table tennis and make it accessible to everyone in the Cork community. We believe in fostering a love for the game and helping individuals develop their skills, whether they are beginners or advanced players.

Our Vision

We aim to create a vibrant table tennis community in Ireland where people can come together to play, learn, and compete. Our vision is to see table tennis thrive as a popular and inclusive sport in our city, with opportunities for social play, training, and competitive matches.

What We Offer

  • Training Sessions: Regular coaching sessions for different skill levels, from beginners to advanced players.
  • Tournaments: Exciting tournaments and competitions for members to showcase their skills and compete in a friendly environment.
  • Social Events: Fun and engaging social events to build camaraderie and strengthen our community.
  • Online Resources: Access to a variety of online resources, including rules, tips, and our very own ping pong simulation game.

Our Locations

We have multiple locations across Ireland in which we recommend to, equipped with top-notch facilities to ensure a great playing experience.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of professionals works tirelessly to create a supportive and enjoyable environment for all our members.

  • Percy Moran: Founder and Head Coach – With over 20 years of experience, Percy is passionate about coaching and developing table tennis talent.
  • Manoell Lea: Community Outreach Coordinator – Manoel ensures that our club remains connected to the local community and accessible to everyone.
  • Alex Bruno: Event Manager – Alex organizes our tournaments and social events, ensuring they run smoothly and are enjoyable for all participants.

Join Us

Whether you’re looking to play casually, improve your skills, or compete at a higher level, P1P0 is the place for you. Join us today and be part of a thriving table tennis community in Cork!
