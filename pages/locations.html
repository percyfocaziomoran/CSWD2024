Where to play in Ireland?

List of places or clubs that you can play

Here we have the best-rated locations of the six main cities in the country!

Dublin

Loop Table Tennis Club

One of the best rated places with 5 stars over 52 reviews on Google! According to their website, the club was founded in 2015 by Na Ning, a professional player in his native China.

Loop Table Tennis Club, Ardscoil La Salle, Raheny Rd, Donaghmede, Raheny, Dublin, D05 Y132, Ireland.

Cork

Beech Hill Table Tennis Club

A friendly table tennis club in the heart of the city known for its incredible group of Paralympic players. They are engaged in many competitions, and you can play if you are a beginner!

Terence MacSwiney Sports Hall, Hollyhill Lane, Knocknaheeny, Cork, Ireland, Eircode: T23W880

Limerick

Limerick City Centre

Limerick have some public outdoors spots very well located where in the summer many players enjoy the game all day long. This locations is right besides the Arthur's Quay park!

Honan's Quay, behind the Limerick Citizen Innovation Lab, M97C+WF Limerick

Galway

CBC Table Tennis Galway

The Commercial Boat Club in Galway is a friendly place to play. The club also offers many others activities. Do not hesitate to play a game if you are in Galway anytime!

Waterside, Woodquay, Galway, Ireland, H91 FH61

Waterford

Table Tennis Stars Waterford

With years of expertise in the game, their passionate team is dedicated to providing a top-quality experience for players of all levels. From beginners to seasoned pros!

Waterford Business Park, Ballynaneashagh, Waterford, X91 HW57, Ierland

Athlone

Athlone Table Tennis Club

A fun place to play regardless of your level and age. The Club have high-quality tables as well as equipment. If you are around Athlone you should give it a go and meet the players!

St Marys Hall, Northgate St, Athlone, Co. Westmeath
