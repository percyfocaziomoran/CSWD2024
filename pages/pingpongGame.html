Rules!

The game has 5 rounds. The first and second go until 5 points, while the third and forth go until 3 points.
The fifth, and last round, has only 2 points to be scored. But be careful, each rounds gets faster, and the ball always respawn randomly from the side of the last scored point.

Good luck!
